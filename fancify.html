A Heritage in Crisis

Since the days of Noah's Ark to the 32 Dickin Medal heroes of WWII, pigeons have been our ultimate messengers. Today, this bond is breaking.

83%

Decline in UK Clubs

In 1980, there were 60,000 competitive pigeon organizations in the UK. Today, there are less than 10,000. Without digital intervention, a thousand-year-old knowledge of animal husbandry will vanish.

Loss of History

The stories of the French Resistance and the founding of Reuters news agency risk being forgotten by modern youth.

Disconnection from Nature

Fewer opportunities for suburban and rural youth to engage in meaningful outdoor learning and animal responsibility.
